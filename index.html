The V.A.L.U.E. Framework for AI Tool Selection
V
Value — What problem does it solve? (Problem & Task Alignment)
A
Accessibility — How easy it is to learn and use (Technical & Security Assessment)
L
Leverage — How much time and effort it saves (Integration & Scalability)
U
Understanding — Can I trust the output? (Risk & Confidence Balance)
E
Evolution — Will it stay useful as AI changes? (Vendor Viability & Support)
There are more than 14,000 active AI tools available to users in 2026. Selection of tools is an important step when creating an effective workflow.

Prompt engineering basics
Four ingredients turn a vague ask into a useful answer
🧑
Role
Tell it who to act as. "You are an operations manager."
Set the persona
🎯
Task
State exactly what you want done. "Draft a vendor follow-up."
Be specific
🗂️
Context
Give the background it needs. "We're 2 weeks past deadline."
Give background
📐
Format
Specify how to get it back. "3 short paragraphs, friendly tone."
Shape the output
